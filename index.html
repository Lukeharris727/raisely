Dash Digital Cash

Dash is a popular cryptocurrency that is a bit more unique than just a digital coin. Every month when Dash releases new currency into circulation, a portion of this currency is set aside to fund various projects pitched by members of the community. Members submit project proposals that could potentially help the Dash community, and these proposals are voted on by the community. If a proposal exceeds a certain amount of 'Yes' votes, then it gets the funding that it requested. This business model is called a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).


What I would change is Dash's marketing strategy. Cryptocurrencies still suffer from a negative stigma, so if I were Dash, I would ramp up my internet marketing campaign to try and spread awareness that Dash (ad cryptocurrency in general) can be much more than just "internet funny money".
